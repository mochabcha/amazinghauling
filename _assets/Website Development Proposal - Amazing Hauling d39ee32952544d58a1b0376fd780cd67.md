## Project Scope

This engagement includes complete website development, branding refresh, and ongoing technical support to position Amazing Hauling for sustainable growth in the Jacksonville market and surrounding areas.

### Website Development

**Core Features:**

- **Service-Focused Homepage** highlighting dependability guarantees and competitive advantages
- **Services Page** detailing all hauling capabilities (dirt, rocks, asphalt, aggregates, milling)
- **Projects Gallery** showcasing trucks in action with professional worksite photography
- **About Page** featuring family/team information and company story
- **Service Area Information** covering Northeast Florida (Duval, Clay, Nassau, St. John's, St. Mary's)
- **Contact Forms** for quote requests and general inquiries
- **Subcontractor Resources** with downloadable packets and forms
- **Mobile-Responsive Design** optimized for all devices
- **Metrics Section** displaying key statistics (client satisfaction, safety record, projects completed)

**Technical Implementation:**

- Professional domain setup ([amazinghauling.com](http://amazinghauling.com) or similar)
- Custom email forwarding ([antoine@amazinghauling.com](mailto:antoine@amazinghauling.com), [nikki@amazinghauling.com](mailto:nikki@amazinghauling.com))
- SSL security certificate
- Fast-loading, optimized performance
- Professional photography integration
- Call-to-action sections throughout

### Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

- Keyword optimization for "dirt hauling," "materials hauling," "dump trucks" + geographic terms
- Sitemap structure for Google indexing
- Meta descriptions and title tags
- Local business schema markup
- Mobile optimization for search rankings

### Web Presence Setup

- Google Business Profile creation and optimization
- Bing Places listing
- Yelp business listing
- Additional directory submissions
- Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all platforms

### Logo Refresh

- Updated Amazing Hauling logo design featuring truck emblem
- Vector file creation for scalability (billboard-ready)
- Simplified design for better translation across mediums
- Multiple format deliverables (PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF)
- Brand color palette and usage guidelines

### Business Card Design

- Professional business card design featuring new logo
- High-quality print specifications
- Textured/embossed finish recommendations
- Print-ready files for ordering
- Design optimized for networking impact
